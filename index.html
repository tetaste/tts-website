Company Overview

Built on 12 Years of Real-World Cloud Expertise

TTS Tech Consulting is a global IT consulting services provider based in Houston, Texas. We pride ourselves on delivering high value to clients through integrated, reliable, responsive and cost-effective solutions.

Over the course of our company's formation — backed by 12 years of industry experience — we have been a trusted partner to small, medium, and large enterprises, enabling businesses to overcome critical IT challenges that slow growth.

We hold profound experience and expertise in complex IT implementation projects, integrating emerging technologies in dynamic environments. Our cutting-edge services make us a seamless extension of our clients' internal teams.

TTS's services include Cloud Adoption, Cloud Administration, Cloud Architecture, Cloud Support, Data Architecture, Security Architecture, Database Administration, and Security Advisory. We gauge our success by customer success alone.
